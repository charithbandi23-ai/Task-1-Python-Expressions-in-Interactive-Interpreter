1.4 Circumference of Circle

Scenario: Calculate the circumference of a circle.

Algorithm:

1. Initialize the variables radius, pi.

2. Calculate circumference: circumference = 2 * pi * radius

3. Print the circumference.

Code:
radius = 5
Pi = 3.14159
circumference = 2 * Pi * radius
print("Circumference:", circumference)

Output:
Circumference of Circle: 43.98226
